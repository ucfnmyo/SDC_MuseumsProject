The MET Art Visualiser is an interactive tool that allows the user to navigate over 400,000 works of art from the MET Art collection on a map. Various informative tools are presented to the user to enable endless exploration of over 200 types of artwork

How use the dashboard:

  1. From the bart chart, select the time range you wish to explore, simply click and drag on the chart to select your parameters.
  2. The 'Select Category' dropdown menu enables you to filter through over 200 categories of artwork
  3. If you are interested in examining artworks of a certain medium (materials and techniques used in the artwork), select your desired filters from the 'Select Medium' dropdown menu
  4. After selecting your filters, the map will update according to the number of artworks in each country, select the country you're interested in and a table summarizing the artworks wil appear in the dashboard
  5. In the artwork display table, select the specific artwork you are interested in by clicking its name
